5.5 Cleaned-Label Evaluation

Source predictions:
results/5.3 AST/predictions.csv

Manual review file:
results/5.5 Error Analysis/data_cleaning_candidates.csv

Results:
- Original test labels: accuracy 0.7069, macro-F1 0.7062, n=1600
- Strict cleaned 8-class labels: accuracy 0.8366, macro-F1 0.8336, n=1597
- Cleaned labels with unresolved kept original: accuracy 0.8350, macro-F1 0.8320, n=1600

Cleaning rule:
- In-domain subjective labels replace original test labels.
- Experimental/Speech is treated as Experimental.
- Pure Speech labels are excluded from the strict 8-class cleaned-label metric.

Detailed per-class results are in cleaned_vs_original_per_class.csv and cleaned_vs_original_per_class.png.
